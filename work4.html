GIFTD APP

The Challenge:

Create a responsive interactive web and mobile application prototype for Giftd; an app which facilitates an easy alternative for parents gifting their children’s teachers and staff.

The Solution:

The design process included extensive qualitative and quantitative research methods guided by task-successs and happiness metrics, sketching, prototyping and testing which resulted in an easy-to-use interface. The muted colors and simplicity of Giftd were applied to envoke the company’s young contemporary and clean feel.
