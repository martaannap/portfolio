ZENR

The Challenge

Create an application that works in conjunction with a wearable smart product. Adhere to Google Material Design.

The Solution

Zenr decreases stress and increases productivity through fundamental stress management and relaxation training. The application works by monitoring heart rate and respiration via smart-watch.
