CITY BOTANICS

The Challenge:

Create an application that contributes to a chosen cause based on one of the Global Goals for Sustainable Development.

The Solution

An application that guides and educates city dwellers in finding the right indoor plants that help contribute in offsetting air pollution, and efficiently creating better indoor air quality.
