ADA ROUND FONT

The Challenge:

Design a serif typeface and matching dingbats.

The Solution:

Inspired by modern minimal geometric fonts, Ada Round keeps a consistent stroke weight for symmetry, combined with a bracketed serif. To maintain a minimal and clean look of the font, there is only 1 serif per letter.
